Vyrde Venture Group, LLC

A financial and information nexus for the cannabis industry.

Our Opportunity

Problem worth solving

The different regulatory frameworks of each state, fragmented infrastructure, and lack of product standardization has made it challenging for businesses, individuals and government entities to facilitate industry expansion.

Our solution

State regulatory information, company licensure status' and standardized chemical profiles will be consolidated on our blockchain-based platform. We will also leverage our cannabis fund to propel promising cannabis ventures.

Sales and Marketing

Sales channels

Vyrde currently maintains a database of licensed cannabis companies in every state where cannabis operations are legal. Vyrde is also actively vetting licensed cannabis companies and identifying the most promising ventures to allocate funding to.

Marketing activities

The marketing strategy is centered around building a trusted brand through investment performance. Marketing efforts are targeted towards direct outreach to pre-selected projects and investors.

Forecast

Revenue streams

The Vyrde platform will generate revenue through information verification and syndication. Vyrde pursues investments that can return greater than 30% re-investable revenue annually. This revenue will multiply as it is re-invested over 7 years.
